Screen Filler Screenprint!

Hand painted screenfiller stencil with blue ink on palm. Four color print on paper.

This print project was to practice painting liquid screen filler directly onto the screen using our own design. I chose a hand to reflect the hand-made aspect of this piece, where I printed four layers on color. I used a blue ink as the shadow in the palm to compliment the yellow shape in the background.
