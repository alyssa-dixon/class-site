Pecha Kucha Poster!

Poster design for VCU's graphic design department Pecha Kucha event.

This poster design is for the graphic design department, where students will deliver 3 minute informal talks about their practice, work, & interests. The focus for this design was to create a reflection of sequence, where the cascading circles that pass the title show a pattern of order. A paper like texture overlays the turquoise shapes and bitmap patterned background behind the words "Pecha Kucha."
