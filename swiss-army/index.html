Linolium Block Print!

Relief print using a hand carved design from a linolium block. A small swiss army knife sits in the center with red ink in the background.

This print was a personal project to play with colored ink in a single subject. I carved this image of a swiss army knife with a linolium block, practicing relief printing as a chance to step away from digital media amongst my school semester.
