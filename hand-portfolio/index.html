Print Practice & Theory Collection

Handmade portfolio contains 8 screenprints of hand made type excerpts with hand sketches of unfolding fist. Screenprints are bookended with carved relief print that show three hand forms in black ink.

This was the final project of our Print Practice & Theory class. Our goal was to create this final piece as a wrap up of our research in two different types of printmaking processes. I chose relief printing as well as screenprinting for this piece, where I dove into a writing practice to describe handmade processes and their significance in my practice of design and print. I made the portfolio to contain all the prints and to be picked up during exhibition.
