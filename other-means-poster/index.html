Other Means Lecture Poster

Poster design for VCU's graphic design department Other Means guest lecture event.

This poster design is inspired by the studio Other Means and their recent design for GOD at p-r-i-m-e-t-i-m-e event. Using bold complimentary colors, my aim was to create a subject that plays into the text details, like the potato image in the GOD at p-r-i-m-e-t-i-m-e post. By angling the imagery of the egg toast figure, I was able to bring the viewer back to the information at the bottom for the lecture happening through VCU on October 31.
